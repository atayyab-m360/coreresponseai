The Framework

The Five CORE AI Pillars

Every business is evaluated across five dimensions. Together, they determine how visible, credible, and competitive your business is — to both humans and AI platforms.

Pillar 01
🔍
Discoverability
Can people and AI platforms clearly find and understand your business? We evaluate your website, Google Business Profile, directory listings, and AI platform presence.
Pillar 02
⭐
Trust
Does your business have the credibility signals that convert visitors into clients? Reviews, testimonials, proof, authority, and social validation all matter.
Pillar 03
🎯
Conversion
Does your website and customer journey turn visitors into leads, calls, bookings, or buyers? We evaluate your CTAs, messaging clarity, and conversion flow.
Pillar 04
⚡
Automation
Are repetitive tasks — follow-ups, reviews, intake, and communication — handled efficiently? We identify where automation can save you time and prevent lost revenue.
Pillar 05
🧠
Intelligence
Is your business using data, CRM, AI tools, and reporting to make better decisions? We evaluate your current systems and identify where AI can give you a real edge.
