🏛️ Chamber of Commerce ✓ Live Project

Transforming LeadShare from Paper-Based Networking into a Connected Digital Experience

CORE AI transformed a paper-based networking process into a mobile-first business system that helps members connect before, during, and after every meeting.

🏛️
Prince William Chamber of Commerce — LeadShare System
Project photo coming soon
The Challenge

A great networking program held back by outdated processes.

The Prince William Chamber's LeadShare program brought local business owners together every week. But the experience around the meeting — checking in, finding members, sharing leads, tracking attendance — was entirely manual. The friction wasn't obvious. It had just become normal.

📝
Paper clipboard sign-in at every meeting
🔍
No searchable member directory
👤
Members didn't know who else was attending
👈
Members often left before networking opportunities
📞
No easy way to contact another member
🖳
No visibility into open seats within LeadShare groups
📊
No attendance reporting for leadership
🚫
Difficult to identify visitors or overdue members
🔔
No announcements between meetings
📅
No central place to share events or updates
“The problem wasn't paper sign-in. The problem was a disconnected member experience.”
Our Discovery

We started by mapping friction — not by writing code.

CORE AI did not begin by building software. Before any design or development work began, we mapped the entire LeadShare member journey — from before a member arrives to after they leave.

We asked: where does the experience break down? Where are members frustrated? Where is the staff doing manual work that a system should handle? Where are opportunities being missed?

The goal wasn't to digitize a clipboard. The goal was to improve the member experience before, during, and after every meeting — and give Chamber leadership the visibility they needed to manage the program effectively.

Only after that discovery did the right solution become clear.

The Solution

A mobile-first LeadShare Command Center — built around the member experience.

Every feature in the system was chosen because it solved a specific friction point identified during discovery. Nothing was added for the sake of technology.

Before vs. After

What changed for members and leadership.

Area Before After
Check-In Paper sign-in clipboard QR code self check-in — seconds, no staff needed
Attendance Tracking Manual clipboard records Automatic digital attendance history
Member Directory No searchable directory Searchable mobile directory with contact details
Member Contact Manual networking, no easy way to connect One-tap calls and emails from any member's profile
Announcements No system between meetings Digital announcements available anytime
Leadership Visibility No attendance reporting Real-time attendance dashboard for leadership
Open Seats No visibility into available industry seats Open seat availability visible to all members
Visitor Management Difficult to identify visitors or track visits Visitor tracking with automatic 2-visit limits
The Outcome

A stronger foundation for member engagement and Chamber growth.

Specific metrics will be documented as the system matures. These are the meaningful business outcomes the system was designed to deliver.

👤
Improved Member Experience
Members arrive, check in, and connect with each other in seconds — from their own phones.
⚡
Faster Networking
Finding another member and reaching out takes one tap instead of a clipboard search.
💬
Easier Communication
Announcements and events reach members between meetings — not just during them.
📊
Better Leadership Visibility
Chamber staff see real-time attendance, visitor activity, and member status at a glance.
🕐
Reduced Admin Work
Manual check-in, attendance tracking, and member lookups are now handled by the system.
🚀
Foundation for Future Automation
The system is built to connect with CRM, payment, and renewal automation as the program grows.
